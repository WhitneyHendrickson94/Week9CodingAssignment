What We Do

Always One More

Our mottos is - "Always One More". We believe in perservering in our efforts as there is always one more child or young adult we can reach. We offer a variety of programs in the Inner City Ministry. They are all geared towards nurturing, loving and developing young souls by teaching basic life skills, vocational skills, introducing them to scripture in the bible, and reinforcing positive behavior in a safe, welcoming evironment. See the table below for futher descriptions of our available programs.

Program Dates Ages Involved Description
Bus Teams Year Long Children Ages 3-18 Our bus teams provide not only monitor safety and provide transportation to our programs,but use that time to work together as a team to make meaninful connections, teach and mentor our youth.
Camp 2 Weeks in the Summer Children Ages 10-17 years old Spend two weeks at Camp HyLake providing mentorship, entertainment, and guidance for the youth in our programs.
Conqueror's Teen Program Year Long Teens Ages 14-18 Teenage years can be filled with many social and emotional struggles, especially depending on the current state of the responsibilities teens in our program may be facing in their home environment. In our Conqueror's Program, you can provide mentorship to a teen by providing them with basic encouragement, helping them with basic life skills, encouraging completion of high school, providing guidance in conflict resolution, and many more areas.
Learning Centers Children Ages 3-15 September-May on Monday Evenings Participate at a local church building at our learning centers during the school year. On these nights, we feed our youth and provide bible lessons and crafts.
Vocational Training Year Long Teens Ages 16-18 Provide mentorship to teens as they begin choosing career paths. Provide guidance on basic resumee writing and interview skills, and well as encouragement in diving into the workforce in the future.
